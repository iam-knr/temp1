The Strategy

A two-phase engine built to compound.

Phase 1 delivers results in 30 days. Phase 2 builds an unstoppable presence that grows month over month.

01 / META

Meta Awareness Ads

Beautiful ad creative served to expecting and new parents in JP Nagar, BTM, Jayanagar, and Koramangala. Build recall so when it's time to book, they think of AdorableShots first.

02 / META

Meta Lead Gen Ads

Built-in forms collect name, phone, and baby's due date directly inside Instagram/Facebook — a ready-to-call lead list without requiring a website visit.

03 / GOOGLE

Google Search Ads

Capture high-intent searches like "newborn photographer Bangalore" at the exact moment someone is ready to book. Your studio at the top of Google, immediately.

04 / GOOGLE

Google Demand Gen Ads

Visual creative across YouTube, Gmail, and Google Discover. Creates desire before the customer even knows they want to book — ideal for a photography studio where the work sells itself.

05 / PAGES

2 Dedicated Ad Landing Pages

One for newborn shoots, one for maternity/kids. Mobile-first, conversion-obsessed pages where every element has a single job: turn a click into a booking inquiry.

01 / WEBSITE

Website Rebuild (Multi-Page)

A professionally designed site with portfolio galleries, clear package pages, WhatsApp integration, Google review display, and a seamless booking flow. Your 24/7 sales machine.

02 / PAID

Paid Ads — Continued

Ongoing management and scaling of Phase 1 campaigns — optimising for lower cost-per-lead, higher quality inquiries, and seasonal spikes like Diwali and maternity season.

03 / SEO

SEO (Monthly Retainer)

Page optimisation, keyword-rich content, and authority building so AdorableShots ranks on Page 1 for "newborn photographer Bangalore" — free, organic traffic your competitors pay for.

04 / SEO

Programmatic SEO

Hundreds of targeted pages at once — for every combination your audience searches. "Baby shoot studio BTM Layout," "maternity photographer Jayanagar." Dominates search at scale.

05 / LOCAL

Hyperlocal SEO

Target JP Nagar, BTM, Jayanagar, Koramangala, Banashankari individually. Most of your clients live within 5–10 km. Own every micro-market around your studio.

06 / GMB

Google My Business Optimisation

Full GMB profile optimisation: categories, photos, Q&A, posts, review strategy, and booking links. Own the Map Pack in South Bangalore — 40%+ of local clicks happen here.
